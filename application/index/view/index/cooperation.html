Established in 2006, baoshilong home has two brands, baoshilong and sofitello. It is an integrated home enterprise integrating R & D, design, production and sales. Over the past 15 years, we have always adhered to the strategic positioning of "professional, original and quality", focusing on the breakthrough and innovation of customized ceiling, integrated wall, integrated ceiling and other plates in the field of home decoration. Through strong brand marketing ability, promote and lead the whole ceiling industry to "assembly, integration, customization, intelligent" transformation.

 

宝仕龙家居创建于2006年，旗下拥有宝仕龙、索菲尼洛两个品牌，是一家集研发、设计、生产、销售于一体的集成家居企业。十五年来，始终秉持“专业、原创、品质”的战略定位，专注于家装领域中定制吊顶、集成墙面、集成吊顶等板块的突破创新。通过强大的品牌营销能力，推动并引领整个吊顶产业向“装配式、集成化、定制化、智能化”转型。
